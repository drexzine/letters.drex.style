Drex is a place for **learning your craft, among others**. You'll gather with a few people in a Circle, take on a creative prompt, reflect on what you make, turn that into a scrappy zine, and share that zine with the world. Keep practicing **with your Circle**, and you'll **grow in your craft**.

Drex is built from **Situated Learning** theory. [Circle Letters](/) is a space for the community to **make sense** of how learning and growth is happening at Drex. When you Request an Invite to Drex, you'll start to recieve the letters in your email inbox.
