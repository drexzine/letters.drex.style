This is the **third surface**.

There's [drex.style](https://drex.style), where you find the door. There's the everyday,
out loud, on [Instagram](https://instagram.com/drexcircles). And then there's this — the
quieter one. The notebook. The place where the thinking gets written down long enough to
be read twice.

Drex is a community where people gather in small circles around their craft and make zines
together. Style is a practice, not a purchase. We exist to help people bring their gifts
into the world — *together*, not alone. That belief needs more room than a caption. So it
lives here.

## Who's writing

I'm **Chielo Mbaezue**, co-founder of Drex. I write the canon (how the thing is meant to
work), the lore (where it came from and what it's becoming), and the occasional letter to
no one in particular that turns out to be for someone in particular.

These are long. They're meant to be. But they should read cleanly — on a train, on a
phone, in a waiting room. If an essay ever fatigues you before it earns it, that's a bug.
Tell me: [hi@drex.style](mailto:hi@drex.style).

## Already running a practice?

If you gather a community around a craft and want the Drex way inside it, invite me in. I'll
